Paper Jungle

The morphological characteristics of jungle plants come to life using cardboard which is extracted, and simulated, and restored. Fully grasp the characteristics of different gram papers, design and make plates according to the characteristics of plants, and cut, indent, fold, twist, wrap and other processes, and then paste and assemble into different plant shapes. The result is a patchwork of various delightful paper plant art, and a paper forest is born. The tropical jungle created by Nan Paper is refreshingly natural. The white jungle is pure and soft, and the pink jungle is dreamy and beautiful. To be immersed in this jungle would be to hear the leaves rustling in the wind and the little rabbit whisper, to see the smile of the giraffe and the flamingo running to take flight…
