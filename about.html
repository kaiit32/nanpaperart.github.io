Our Team



Established in 2012, NanPaper Art has made its mark in China as a high-end paper design brand. Synonymous with the most intuitive, Nan Paper Art uses an expert eye to find the perfect combination of space, color and aesthetics, continuously pushing the boundaries of existing materials, techniques and qualities. The company’s aim is the ultimate pursuit of beauty in handcrafted art and inheritance of paper art. The creations are concerned with narrative, they seek to lead you into a story or environment and invite you to be moved by this. Nan Paper Art not only represents the highest level of domestic paper design, but also is leading the wave of Chinese paper production and design.

Over the past ten years, NanPaper Art has increased its brand awareness, raised sustainable paper art concepts, leveled up the team size and its capabilities of research and development, connected with a wide range of high-status customer groups. Focusing on creating original paper artwork of Chinese symbol, NanPaper Art has established an extraordinary Chinese paper art trend and given a new life to traditional Chinese paper art.

The company has worked with hundreds of companies and organisations from small projects to big creations. It has become the top paper design company for many internationally renowned brands both in China and worldwide. NanPaper Art has been designated as the only paper supplier in China by international brands such as Hermes, Omega, Louis XIII, Porsche, Mercedes Benz, Lancôme, Estee Lauder and Chanel. It has also established long-term and stable cooperative relations with many well-known real-estate companies such as Vanke, Rong Chuang, OCT, Greentown, Greenland Hong Kong and etc.

Its vast scope of services includes top luxury weddings, window display, space decoration design, high-end public relations activities, TV show stage sets, art installations and etc.
